Design Portfolio Guidelines

What is a design portfolio?
- A design portfolio is a book in which an engineer will formally document, in chronological order, all of his/her work that is associated with a specific design project.

Why do we keep them?
- Typically required by companies
- Recognized as a legal document in patent activities
- Prove the origin of an idea
- Prove diligence in turning an idea into a solution
- Prove when an idea became a working solution


Twine Interactive Fiction

Reflection:
     When playing the game, consider the potential impact that technology will have on the future, such as issues of general human depednence or the dangers of exploited development. To what extent do you think is okay for a person to rely on technology? Is there bound to be a decrease in general human capablities if technology is there to do the work for you? In a world that is constantly developing, how far will companies go to maintain control or what security protocols will be needed to prevent new ideas from being exploited.
